# 🧚 Fairy PWA – Install Guide

## How to put Fairy on your Android phone

### Step 1 – Make a free GitHub account
1. Go to **github.com**
2. Click **Sign up** and make a free account

### Step 2 – Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** button (top right) → **New repository**
2. Name it exactly: `fairy-app`
3. Set it to **Public**
4. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3 – Upload the files
1. On your new repo page, click **uploading an existing file**
2. Drag ALL these files into the box:
   - `index.html`
   - `manifest.json`
   - `sw.js`
   - `icon-192.png`
   - `icon-512.png`
   - `favicon.ico`
3. Click **Commit changes**

### Step 4 – Turn on GitHub Pages
1. Go to **Settings** tab in your repo
2. Scroll down to **Pages** (left sidebar)
3. Under **Source**, select **main** branch
4. Click **Save**
5. Wait ~1 minute, then your URL will be:
   `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/fairy-app`

### Step 5 – Install on Android
1. Open **Chrome** on your Android phone
2. Go to your GitHub Pages URL above
3. Tap the **3 dots menu** (top right)
4. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
5. Tap **Add** — Fairy's icon appears on your home screen! 🎉

### Done! 
Tap the Fairy icon to open her like a real app — fullscreen, no browser bar!
